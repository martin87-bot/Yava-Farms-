Agrisisters Initiative

Empowering women through climate-smart agriculture

Overview

The Agrisisters Initiative is a flagship women-led program of Yava Farms and Agricultural Consultancy. It empowers rural women with access to land, climate-smart farming skills, modern inputs, and structured markets.

Agrisisters responds directly to the intersecting challenges of climate change, rural poverty, and gender inequality in agriculture.

What We Do

  • Provide access to land through cooperative arrangements
  • Hands-on training in crop production and agribusiness
  • Supply quality seeds, inputs, and irrigation support
  • Guarantee market access through offtake partnerships
  • Promote savings, reinvestment, and cooperative growth

Impact Goals

Increase availability of nutritious, locally produced food in rural and urban markets.
