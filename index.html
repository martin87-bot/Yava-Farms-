About Us

Yava Farms and Agricultural Consultancy is a Ghanaian agribusiness committed to climate-smart agriculture, food security, and inclusive rural development. We work at the intersection of sustainable production, youth and women empowerment, and innovative financing models.

Our work responds directly to climate change challenges by restoring degraded land, improving water efficiency, and building resilient farming communities.

What We Do

Crop Production

Open-field and greenhouse vegetable production using certified seeds and climate-resilient practices.

Agricultural Consultancy

Technical advisory services, farm planning, irrigation design, and production management support.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Implementation of initiatives such as Agrisisters and cooperative farming programs.

Climate-Smart Solutions

Soil restoration, efficient water use, and sustainable farming models that respond to climate change.

Upcoming Initiatives
