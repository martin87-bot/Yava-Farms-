Agrisisters Initiative

Empowering women through climate-smart agriculture

Overview

The Agrisisters Initiative is a flagship women-led program of Yava Farms and Agricultural Consultancy. It is designed to empower rural women with access to land, climate-smart farming skills, modern inputs, and structured markets.

Agrisisters responds directly to the intersecting challenges of climate change, rural poverty, and gender inequality in agriculture.

Why Agrisisters?

Women at the Center

Women form the backbone of Ghana’s food system but often lack access to land, finance, and training. Agrisisters puts women first.

Climate-Smart Farming

Participants are trained in sustainable production practices that improve yields while protecting soil and water resources.

Income & Dignity

The program creates reliable income streams, improves household food security, and strengthens women’s economic independence.

What We Do

  • Provide access to land through cooperative arrangements
  • Deliver hands-on training in crop production and agribusiness
  • Supply quality seeds, inputs, and irrigation support
  • Guarantee market access through offtake partnerships
  • Promote savings, reinvestment, and cooperative growth

Impact Goals

Women Empowered

Support hundreds of rural women to become successful climate-smart farmers.

Land Restored

Rehabilitate degraded land using sustainable soil and water management practices.

Food Security

Increase availability of nutritious, locally produced food for communities.

Partner With Agrisisters

Agrisisters welcomes partnerships with donors, foundations, CSR programs, and development agencies seeking high-impact, gender-responsive climate solutions.
