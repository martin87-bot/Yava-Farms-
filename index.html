Yava Farms

About Us

Yava Farms and Agricultural Consultancy is a Ghanaian agribusiness committed to climate-smart agriculture, food security, and inclusive rural development.

We respond to climate change by restoring degraded land, improving water efficiency, and building resilient farming communities.

What We Do

Crop Production

Open-field and greenhouse vegetable production using certified seeds and climate-resilient practices.

Agricultural Consultancy

Farm planning, irrigation design, training, and production management support.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Implementation of Agrisisters and cooperative farming programs.

Climate-Smart Solutions

Soil restoration, efficient water use, and sustainable food systems.
