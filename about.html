About Yava Farms

Climate-smart agriculture for people, planet, and prosperity

Who We Are

Yava Farms & Agricultural Consultancy is a Ghanaian agribusiness and social enterprise committed to transforming agriculture through climate-smart practices, innovation, and inclusive rural development.

Established to respond to climate change, food insecurity, and rural unemployment, Yava Farms integrates production, training, consultancy, and community-based initiatives to build resilient food systems across Ghana.

Our Mission

To empower farmers, women, and youth through climate-smart agriculture, practical training, access to markets, and sustainable agribusiness solutions that improve livelihoods and food security.

Our Vision

To become a leading climate-smart agribusiness and consultancy in West Africa, driving resilient food systems, environmental restoration, and inclusive economic growth.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We promote practices that protect soil, water, and ecosystems for future generations.

Inclusion

We intentionally empower women and youth as key drivers of agricultural transformation.

Innovation

We apply modern technology, data, and climate-smart solutions to traditional farming systems.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, accountability, and respect for communities and partners.
