The Story Behind the Painting

“Tears of the Forest” depicts two trees that have been cut down. One tree weeps. The other survives by collecting those tears in a watering can and using them to nourish itself.

This is not just art. It is a warning.

Yava Farms is transforming this artwork into action — turning emotion into food, jobs, and climate resilience.

Our Climate-Smart Solution

Irrigated vegetable and rice farming
Greenhouse and open-field systems
Youth & women cooperatives (70% women)
Solar-powered energy & cold storage
Affordable modern mud housing
Future fish farming integration

Art funds agriculture. Agriculture restores dignity.
