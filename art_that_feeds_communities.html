The Story Behind the Painting

“Tears of the Forest” depicts two trees that have been cut down. One tree weeps. The other survives by collecting those tears in a watering can and using them to nourish itself.

This is not just art. It is a warning. Climate change has reached a point where nature is forced to survive on its own suffering.

Instead of allowing this artwork to hang silently, Yava Farms is transforming it into a catalyst for action — turning emotion into food, jobs, and resilience.
