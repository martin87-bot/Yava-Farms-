The Story Behind the Painting

“Tears of the Forest” depicts two trees that have been cut down. One weeps. The other survives by collecting those tears to nourish itself.

This is not just art — it is a warning about climate change and survival.

Yava Farms is transforming this artwork into action, turning emotion into food, jobs, and resilience.
